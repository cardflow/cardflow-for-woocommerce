WordPress plugin for accepting Cardflow gift cards in your WooCommerce shop.

== Description ==

The [Cardflow for WooCommerce](https://cardflow.nl) plugin enables you to accept Cardflow gift cards in your WooCommerce shop.

Your customers can enter their Cardflow gift card code during checkout in the coupon field, and if the coupon is recognized the discount will be applied to the order.
